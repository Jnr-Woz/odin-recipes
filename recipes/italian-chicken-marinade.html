Italian Chicken Marinade

Description

The Italian Chicken Marinade is a simple yet flavorful combination of ingredients that infuses chicken with the delicious essence of Italian cuisine. In a bowl, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper are whisked together to create a well-blended marinade.

The chicken is then placed in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag and coated with the marinade. After refrigerating for at least 30 minutes or up to 24 hours, the chicken absorbs the flavors, resulting in a tantalizing taste. When it's time to cook, the chicken is removed from the marinade and cooked according to your preference, whether it's grilling, baking, or pan-searing.

The end result is tender, juicy chicken infused with the aromatic essence of Italian herbs and spices. It can be served as a delectable main dish or incorporated into various Italian-inspired recipes for a burst of Mediterranean flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-styly salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powdder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish; add chicken breasts and turn to coat. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Remove the chicken from marinade and shake off excess; discard remaining
  4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear about 7 to 8 minutes on each side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
